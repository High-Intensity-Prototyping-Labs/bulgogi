* - Means anything (wildcard)
** - Means anything (recursive)
.. - Vinculum (repeats till exhaustion)
exe - Refers to an executable
lib - Refers to a library
dir - Refers to a directory
abc - Defines a macro (anything else)

# Note on (!) Prohibitive Patterns: everything is prohibited unless mentioned (conservative).

--- Implicit C v0.10.0 Pattern:
exe:
- exe
- lib..
- dir..

lib:
- lib..
- dir..
---

--- The Anything Goes:
'*':
- '**'
---
